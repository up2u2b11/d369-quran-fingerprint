# Experiment 07 — Architecture vs. Words?

**Date:** March 17, 2026
**System:** Special-6
**Unit of analysis:** 114 Surahs × 3 parallel tests

---

## The Fundamental Question

Experiments 1–6 established that the {3,6,9} fingerprint exists.
But:

> **Is the fingerprint in the words of the Quran? Or in how they were divided into 114 Surahs of specific lengths?**

This question defines the nature of the phenomenon:
- If fingerprint is in the **words** → the fingerprint lives in the linguistic content
- If fingerprint is in the **division** → the 114-Surah architecture is the locus

---

## Experimental Design

| Test | Content | Division |
|------|---------|----------|
| A — Reference | Quran words in original order | Quran's Surah lengths |
| B — Shuffled | Quran words shuffled randomly | Same Surah lengths |
| C — Foreign text | Sahih al-Bukhari words | Same Surah lengths |

**Inference logic:**
- A ✅ + B ✗ + C ✗ → fingerprint is in the **word order**
- A ✅ + B ✅ + C ✗ → fingerprint is in **Quranic words** regardless of order
- A ✅ + B ✅ + C ✅ → fingerprint is in the **architecture** (the division itself)

---

## Results (Permutation Test — 3,000 trials)

| Test | {3,6,9} | Pct | p-value |
|------|---------|-----|---------|
| **A — Quran, original order** | **51/114** | **44.7%** | **0.0093 ✅** |
| B — Quran words shuffled | 43/114 | 37.7% | 0.1853 ✗ |
| C — Bukhari with Quran lengths | 39/114 | 34.2% | 0.4497 ✗ |

### Summary

```
Quran (original):     51/114 = 44.7%   p = 0.0093  ✅ significant
Quran (shuffled):     43/114 = 37.7%   p = 0.1853  ✗
Bukhari (Quran arch): 39/114 = 34.2%   p = 0.4497  ✗
```

**The fingerprint is NOT in the division alone** — Bukhari with Quran lengths: 34.2%
**The fingerprint is NOT in the words alone** — Quran shuffled: 37.7%

> **The fingerprint is in the words in the order they were revealed.**
> Words + order = fingerprint. Either one alone is not sufficient.

---

## Significance

This experiment answers Open Door #5 from the published papers.

The answer narrows the search space for further research: any structural explanation must account for why the specific sequence of 78,248 words — and not just their identity — carries the fingerprint.

---

## Note on Test B

The shuffled Quran achieves 37.7%, between the original (44.7%) and Bukhari (34.2%).
This gradient is consistent: Quranic vocabulary itself has a higher {3,6,9} density than Bukhari vocabulary, but the organization of that vocabulary into its specific sequence adds a further signal.
